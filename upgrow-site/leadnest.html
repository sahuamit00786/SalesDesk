Built for teams that live on speed-to-lead

Most CRMs are where leads go to be forgotten. LeadNest is built around one idea: respond first, follow up always, close more.

Auto-distribution and instant notifications mean new leads reach a rep before your competitor even opens their inbox.

Product Tour

Everything your sales day needs

Dashboard

Your sales cockpit

See pipeline value, conversion rates, team activity and revenue trends the moment you log in. No report-building — the numbers that matter are already there.

Leads

Every lead, one inbox

Web forms, landing pages, ads and marketplace enquiries flow into a single, filterable lead list with status, source and owner visible at a glance.

Lead Distribution

Route leads in seconds

Round-robin, territory or rule-based assignment gets every new lead to the right rep instantly — because speed-to-lead wins deals.

Deals Pipeline

Drag, drop, close

Kanban pipelines you can customise per team. Move deals through stages, see value per stage and never lose track of what's stuck.

Quotations

Quotes in minutes

Build branded quotations right inside the deal, with line items, taxes and discounts — then send and track them without leaving LeadNest.

Email Inbox

Two-way email, built in

Connect your mailbox and handle full conversations inside the CRM. Every thread is automatically linked to the right lead and deal.

Campaigns

Know what your marketing earns

Track campaigns and sources end to end, so you know which channels create pipeline — not just clicks.

Workflows

Automate the busywork

A visual workflow editor for follow-ups, assignments, status changes and notifications. Set it once, let it run.

Tasks & Calendar

A day that runs itself

Tasks, calls and meetings in list and calendar views, synced per rep — so every commitment gets honoured.

Calls & Meetings

Every conversation logged

Log calls and meetings against leads and deals, with outcomes and next steps captured while they're fresh.

Teams & Roles

Control who sees what

Integrations & API
