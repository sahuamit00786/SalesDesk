Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Upgrow Ventures ("we", "us", "our") operates the website upgrowventures.co and the LeadNest CRM platform. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us: name, email address, phone number, company name and project details when you fill a form, request a demo, apply for a job, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us by phone, email or WhatsApp.
  • Information collected automatically: standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited and referring URLs, collected via cookies and similar technologies to understand how our website is used.
  • LeadNest customer data: if you use LeadNest, your workspace stores the business data you and your team enter (such as leads, contacts, deals and communications). You control this data; we process it only to provide the service.

2. Google API data and Limited Use

LeadNest offers optional Google integrations. If you choose to connect your Google account, you grant specific scopes via Google's consent screen. We only request what each feature needs:

  • gmail.send — send email from your connected mailbox when you send a message from LeadNest (templates, replies, tracked emails).
  • gmail.modify — read the inbox thread linked to a lead or deal, and update labels (e.g. mark read, archive) so your mailbox stays in sync with LeadNest's threaded view.
  • calendar.events — create, update and read calendar events and Google Meet links for meetings you schedule from LeadNest.
  • userinfo.email / openid — identify which Google account is connected to your LeadNest workspace.

We do not use Gmail or Calendar data for advertising, and we do not sell it. Human access to this data is restricted to what's needed for support, security or legal compliance, and only with your consent or as required by law.

LeadNest's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

You can revoke LeadNest's access to your Google account at any time from your LeadNest account settings or from Google Account permissions. Revoking access stops new syncing; previously synced data already stored in your workspace is handled per section 6 below.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes, demos and support.
  • To provide, maintain, secure and improve our services, including LeadNest.
  • To send service updates and, where you've opted in, marketing communications you can unsubscribe from at any time.
  • To analyse website usage so we can improve content and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (such as hosting, email delivery, analytics and payment processing), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities where required by applicable law or to protect our rights, users or the public.
  • Business transfers — if we are involved in a merger or acquisition, information may transfer as part of that transaction with equivalent protection.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to make the site work and analytics cookies to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect how the site functions.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above, to comply with legal obligations, or to resolve disputes. LeadNest workspace data is retained for the duration of your subscription and deleted or returned on request after termination, subject to our backup cycles. Google Gmail/Calendar data synced into LeadNest is retained only as long as needed for the linked lead, deal or meeting record, or until access is revoked and the cache expires.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards — including encryption in transit, access controls and role-based permissions — to protect information. OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and are never exposed to the client application. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, withdraw consent, or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email us at upgrowventures.co@gmail.com.

9. Third-party links

Our website may link to third-party sites (for example, WhatsApp or social media). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, which we encourage you to review.

10. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

12. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at upgrowventures.co@gmail.com or +91 83685 55482, Upgrow Ventures, New Delhi, India.
